How the Game Works

In Clickfluencer, you have to tap on the phone to earn money and followers. The more you click, the more you earn! Use your earnings to unlock powerful upgrades and exclusive items in the Shop, which will help you level up faster and gain more followers. Click your way to success!

The In-Game Shop

Use your hard-earned points to unlock exciting new music, cool click sounds, and unique cursor styles! Each item you unlock is automatically saved, so you can enjoy your customizations while playing and truly make the game your own.

Tips & Progress

The more you play, the more you achieve in-game! Upgrades are key to boosting your progress. Have fun playing and keep unlocking new possibilities!
